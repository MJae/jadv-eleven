The Professional Criminologists Association of the Philippines (PCAP) Region VIII proudly announced yesterday, September 16, 2024, the top 10 performers in the Criminologists Licensure Examination conducted from July 31 to August 2, 2024. The results were shared via the official Facebook page of the regional branch of PCAP, recognizing outstanding test-takers from various educational institutions across Eastern Visayas.

The announcement aims to honor the dedication and academic excellence of the region’s top performers in the examination. Leading the list is **Christian Molero Serabia** of Burauen Community College in Burauen, Leyte, who achieved the highest score in the region with a rating of **91.00**.

Following Serabia are Ryan Christopher Calamaya Lim (90.95) and Paul Michael Basañes Sabusap (90.60), both from Visayas State University Tolosa, and Ryan Acedillo Hembra (90.70) of Leyte Colleges. The top 10 performers represent a diverse mix of institutions across Leyte and Eastern Samar, all contributing to the region’s growing prestige in the field of criminology.

Here is the complete list of the region's top 10 performers:

1. **Christian Molero Serabia** | 91.00 | Burauen Community College | Burauen, Leyte
2. **Ryan Christopher Calamaya Lim** | 90.95 | Visayas State University Tolosa | Tolosa, Leyte
3. **Ryan Acedillo Hembra** | 90.70 | Leyte Colleges | Tacloban City
4. **Paul Michael Basañes Sabusap** | 90.60 | Visayas State University Tolosa | Tolosa, Leyte
5. **Rey Mark Grado Tenasas** | 90.35 | Burauen Community College | Burauen, Leyte
6. **Ma. Jesabel Servano Samson** | 90.25 | Visayas State University Tolosa | Tolosa, Leyte
7. **Warren Albo Cabangon** | 89.60 | Eastern Samar State University | Borongan City
**Angelito Basario Cablao** | 89.60 | Leyte Colleges | Tacloban City
8. **Daniel Parto Quinagutan** | 89.40 | Leyte Colleges | Tacloban City
9. **Lorna Cabintoy Abejuela** | 89.35 | Burauen Community College | Burauen, Leyte
10. **John Henry Oledan Venigas** | 89.25 | Leyte Colleges | Tacloban City

The recognition of these top performers not only highlights individual achievement but also shines a spotlight on the quality of criminology programs in the region. Educational institutions such as Burauen Community College, Eastern Samar State University, Leyte Colleges, and Visayas State University Tolosa continue to uphold their commitment to producing top-tier professionals in the field.

The successful passers, especially those on the top-performing list, now move closer to securing their roles in public safety, law enforcement, and criminal justice, areas where their expertise will play a pivotal role in maintaining peace and order in society.
